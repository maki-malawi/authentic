Who we are


Authentic Medical Supplies is specialised in dealing with Health Care products.
It is one of the leading suppliers of essential Medical, Laboratory and Surgical
requirements. It is this diversified approach in the distribution of Health Care
products that makes Authentic Medical Supplies to be a premium Pharmaceutical
company in Malawi.
Since its inception in 2009, the company has always been committed to
consistence in offering goods and services of premier quality which offer the best
value for money.
Authentic Medical Supplies is registered with the registrar of companies in Malawi,
the Office of Director of Public Procurement and Pharmacy, Medicines and Poison
Board of Malawi.
